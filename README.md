This is a work-in-progress project by FoxCub (LakeAtTheFiveAmberStars) and YosheW_, based around a far, far sector of Rainworld, where two iterator OCs called Eight Brackets and Transparent Sky reside. Feel like a stranger, trying to find your own path here, is your eternal curse upon entering the sector. From now on, every single creature looks unfamiliar and uncanny. As do you.
